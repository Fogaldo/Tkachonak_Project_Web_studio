What are we doing

Working with the customer. Setting the goals and objectives of the site. Preparation of technical specifications

At the first stage, we listen carefully to the customer, his vision, goals and objectives of the site. After all, when creating a website, we start from its main goal first of all. And they can be different - collect "hot" applications, tell about the action, invite customers to a webinar, talk about their services, help visitors make payments to sites, make online miscalculations, keep the attention of visitors, and more.

We study your product, target audience, do market analysis

Then we look globally at the product. What is the value of your product or service? How does your product make life easier for humanity? The target audience. From this value depends on what site will be in the end. Because everyone's perception is different - men / women, age category, geographic location, mentality, interests and hobbies. The copyright of the site, design, color palette, structure, and so on depends on this. Market analysis is also a very important point at this stage.

We create a prototype and layout of the site

We create a website prototype and design layout in order to agree on all the small but important details at this stage and approve what your website will be like in the end.

We create content

Content is at the heart of everything. No matter how cool and useful your product or service is, it is important to be able to correctly convey its value to a potential client, to show the product from all "beautiful" sides. In addition to the product, content creation is greatly influenced by its target audience. Structure, text, unique photo / video content, calls to action are all important components of a website.

We develop a website

At this stage, layout and programming begins. And the finished design goes into a working project, and all ideas are embodied in reality. At the final stage of layout, an adaptive version of the site is developed.

Setting up and testing the site

The site is almost ready. It remains only to configure and test it. At this stage, we connect the domain, connect the sending of applications from the site, CRM, do internal SEO optimization, connect the necessary widgets (online chats, order a call, etc.), troubleshoot, remove errors, connect Google Analytics, optimize the site speed. .. Done! We give you the finished site.
